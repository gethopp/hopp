You can share your camera during a call in Hopp.

## Hide yourself

Hover over your tile and click the **Hide yourself** button in the bottom right. This hides your self-view while your camera keeps streaming to others. Click the button again to show your self-view.

## Pin and unpin the camera window

Pin the camera window to a small tile in the top-right corner of the screen. Pinning also hides your self-view.

Pin in either of two ways:

- Click the **pin button** in the top-right of the camera window.
- Press `Cmd+P` (macOS) or `Ctrl+P` (Windows / Linux).

<img src="/camera-pin.gif" class="rounded-md max-w-full mx-auto mt-6" alt="Pinning the camera window" />

To unpin, resize the window or press `Cmd+P` / `Ctrl+P` again. The window returns to its centered size and the self-view is shown.
